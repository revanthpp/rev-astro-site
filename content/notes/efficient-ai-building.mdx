You do not need the most expensive AI plan to start building.

A practical workflow:

Start with the idea.
Prototype with local or lower-cost models.
Build, test, and refine.
Use frontier models for final validation where quality really matters.
Then decide what deserves to ship.

Prototype faster. Iterate freely. Spend less.

The fanciest model is not always the best first step.
