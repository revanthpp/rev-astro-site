The demo is not the hard part.

The hard part is what happens after people get excited.

Who owns it?
How is it evaluated?
What happens when it fails?
What does it cost?
Where are the logs?
Who approved the tool call?
Can the workflow stop itself before it burns through the budget?

That is where AI productization really starts.
