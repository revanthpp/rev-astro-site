Trust is not going away.

It is getting re-architected.

As AI systems take on more work, trust has to move from informal human review into system design: audit trails, approvals, evaluations, monitoring, permissions, and clear boundaries.

The future is not uncontrolled autonomy.

It is autonomy with architecture.
