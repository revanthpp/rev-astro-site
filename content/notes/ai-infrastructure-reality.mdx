AI is impressive until the permissions, data, latency, cost, and monitoring show up.

That is not a criticism.

That is the job.

The next generation of AI leaders will need to understand models, but they will also need to understand the system around the model.

Reliability is not a feature you sprinkle on later. It is an architectural choice.
