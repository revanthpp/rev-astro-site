AI demos create momentum.

Production creates accountability.

The gap between the two is where most AI initiatives either mature or quietly disappear.

The real work starts when teams have to answer practical questions: ownership, data access, monitoring, support, cost, evaluation, and governance.

That is not bureaucracy.

That is how AI becomes useful.
