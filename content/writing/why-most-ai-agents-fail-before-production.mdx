Most AI agents do not fail because the model is weak.

They fail because the system around the model is underdesigned.

A useful agentic workflow needs more than a prompt and a tool call. It needs state, permissions, monitoring, fallback behavior, cost awareness, human oversight, and a clear way to stop.

The question is not “Can the agent do the task?”

The better question is:

**Can the system be trusted when the task becomes messy?**
