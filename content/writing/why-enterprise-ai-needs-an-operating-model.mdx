Every organization wants more AI adoption.

Fewer organizations define how AI adoption should actually work.

Who identifies the use cases?
Who validates them?
Who funds them?
Who owns the risk?
Who maintains the workflow after launch?
Who decides when a pilot becomes a product?

AI operating models matter because enthusiasm does not scale by itself.
